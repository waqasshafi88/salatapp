Fajr (2 Rakahs)

First Rakah

First Rakah

Qiyam (Standing)

In the standing position, raise both hands so that your fingertips are in line with the shoulders or ears. Your palms should be facing outward. Then say:

Allahu Akbar

اللهُ أَكْبَـرُ

(Allah is Greatest)

Al Fatihah

Place your hands on your chest, with the right hand over the left. Then read Al Fatihah:

A’oothu billaahi minash shaytan-ir rajeem

أَعُوذُ بِاللّٰهِ مِنَ الشَّيطَٰنِ الرَّجِيم

(I seek refuge with Allah from Satan the accursed.)
Bismil-laahir rah-maanir raheem

بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

(In the name of Allah, the Most Beneficent, the Most Merciful)
Al-hamdu lillaahi rabbil ‘aala-meen

ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ

(Praise be to Allah the Lord of the Worlds)
Ar-rahmaanir-raheem

ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

(The Most Beneficent, the Most Merciful)
Maaliki yaw-mid-deen

مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

(Master of the Day of Judgement)
Iy-yaaka na’-budu wa iy-yaaka nasta-’een

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

(You alone we worship and in You alone we seek help)
Ihdi-nas-siraat al mustaqeem

ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

(Guide us to the straight path)
Siraat-al-la-theena an’amta ‘alay-him

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ

(The way of those whom You have favored)
Ghay-ril magh-doobi ‘alay-him

غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ

(Not the way of those who have earned Your anger)
Walad-daal-leen

وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

(Nor of those who have gone astray)
Aameen

آمِين

(Oh Allah answer our prayer!)

Then read another chapter from the Quran. For example:

Bismil-laahir rah-maanir raheem

بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

(In the name of Allah, the Most Beneficent, the Most Merciful)
Qul huwa-allaahu ahad

قُلْ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ

(Say: He is Allah, (The) One)
Allaahus-samad

ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ

(Allah, the eternal Refuge {the one sought in times of difficulty and need})
Lam ya-lid walam yoo-lad

لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ

(He neither begets, nor is He born})
Walam ya-kul-lahu kufu-wan ahad

وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌۢ

(nor is there to Him any equivalent)

Ruku (Bowing)

In the standing position, raise both hands as to your shoulders. Then say:

Allahu Akbar

اللهُ أَكْبَـرُ

(Allah is Greatest)

Bend down and place hands on knees. Say (3 times):

Sub-haana rab-biyal ‘atheem

سُبْـحانَ رَبِّـيَ الْعَظـيم

(Glory be to my Lord the Supreme)

Second Qiyam (Standing)

Recite this as you stand up from the bowing position. You can place your hands on your chest or on your sides.

Sami’-Allaahu liman hami-dah

سَمِـعَ اللهُ لِمَـنْ حَمِـدَه

(Allah listens to the one who praises Him)
Rabba-naa wa lakal hamd

رَبَّنـا وَلَكَ الحَمْـدُ

(Our Lord, and to You belongs the praise)

Sujood (Two Prostrations)

Go into the prostration (sujood) position. As you go into this position say:

Allahu Akbar

اللهُ أَكْبَـرُ

(Allah is Greatest)

In the prostration position (sujood), ensure:

  • The nose and forehead are touching the ground
  • The two palms are on the floor with fingers together
  • The two knees are on the floor
  • The toes of both feet are upright and not laying flat on the floor

Then say:

Sub-haana rab-biyal ‘alaa

سُبْـحانَ رَبِّـيَ الأَعْلـى

(Glory be to my Lord Most High)

Coming up(Sitting Position:)

While coming up to the sitting position say:

Allahu Akbar

اللهُ أَكْبَـرُ

(Allah is Greatest)

Coming up(Sitting Position Back:)

Sit on the left thigh, with the left foot along the ground and the right foot upright. The toes of the right foot should be facing the Qiblah and the hands should be placed on the knees.

Then say (3 times):

Rab-bigh-firlee

رَبِّ اغْفِـرْ لي

(Oh Allah, forgive me)

Repeat the prostrating position. Then say (3 times):

Sub-haana rab-biyal ‘alaa

سُبْـحانَ رَبِّـيَ الأَعْلـى

(Glory be to my Lord Most High)

Qul a’oothu bi-rabbin-nas

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ

(Say: I seek refuge with the Lord of Mankind)
Malik-in-nas

مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ

(The King of Mankind)
ilaahin-nas

إِلَـٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ

(The God of Mankind)
Min sharril was-waasil khan-naas

مِن شَرِّ ٱلْوَسْوَاسِ ٱلْخَنَّاسِ

(From the evil of the whisperer who withdraws (when one remembers Allah))
Al-lathee yu-was-wisu fee sudoor-in-naas

ٱلَّذِى يُوَسْوِسُ فِى صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ

(Who whispers in the breasts of mankind)
Min-al jin-nati wan-nas

مِنَ ٱلْجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ

(From among the jinn and mankind)

Tashahud(Sitting Position:)

Repeat all the previous steps until sitting after the two prostrating positions. At this point, raise your index finger on your right hand. Recite this:

Atta-hiy-yaatu lilaahi was-sala-waatu wat-tayyi-baatu

التَّحِيّـاتُ للهِ وَالصَّلَـواتُ والطَّيِّـبات

(All compliments, prayers and pure words are due to Allah)
as-salaamu ‘alay-ka ay-yuhan-nabiy-yu

السَّلامُ عَلَيـكَ أَيُّهـا النَّبِـيُّ

(Peace be upon you Oh Prophet)
wa rahmat-ullaahi wa-bara-kaat-uh

وَرَحْمَـةُ اللهِ وَبَرَكـاتُه

(And the mercy of Allah and His blessings)
as-salaamu ‘alay-naa wa ‘alaa ‘ibaa dil-laa his-saliheen

السَّلامُ عَلَيْـناوَعَلـى عِبـادِ للهِ الصَّـالِحـين

(Peace be upon us and on the righteous slaves of Allah)
Ash-hadu al-laa ilaa-ha illal-laah

أَشْـهَدُ أَنْ لا إِلـهَ إِلاّ الله

(I bear witness that there is no God or deity worthy of worship except Allah)
Wa ash-hadu anna Muhammadan ‘abduhu wa rasool-uh

وَأَشْـهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمّـداً عَبْـدُهُ وَرَسـولُه

(And I bear witness that Muhammad is His slave and Messenger)
Wa ash-hadu anna Muhammadan ‘abduhu wa rasool-uh

وَأَشْـهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمّـداً عَبْـدُهُ وَرَسـولُه

(And I bear witness that Muhammad is His slave and Messenger)
Allahum-ma salli ‘ala Muhammad

اللّهُـمَّ صَلِّ عَلـى مُحمَّـد

(Oh Allah, send prayers upon Muhammad)
wa ‘ala aali Muhammad

وَعَلـى آلِ مُحمَّد

(and upon the family of Muhammad)
kamaa sa-layta ‘ala Ibra-heem

كَمـا صَلَّيـتَ عَلـى إبْراهـيمَ

(as You sent prayers upon Ibrahim)
wa ‘ala aali Ibra-heem

وَعَلـى آلِ إبْراهـيم

(and upon the family of Ibrahim)
in-naka hameed-un Majeed

إِنَّكَ حَمـيدٌ مَجـيد

(indeed You are praiseworthy, Most glorious)
Allahum-ma baarik ‘alaa Muhammad

اللّهُـمَّ بارِكْ عَلـى مُحمَّـد

(Oh Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad)
Wa ‘alaa aali Muhammad

وَعَلـى آلِ مُحمَّـد

(and upon the family of Muhammad)
kamaa baa-rakta ‘alaa Ibra-heem

كَمـا بارِكْتَ عَلـى إبْراهـيمَ

(as You sent prayers upon Ibrahim)
wa ‘ala aali Ibra-heem

وَعَلـى آلِ إبْراهيم

(and upon the family of Ibrahim)

إِنَّكَ حَمـيدٌ مَجـيد

(indeed You are praiseworthy, Most glorious)
in-naka hameed-un Majeed

إِنَّكَ حَمـيدٌ مَجـيد

(indeed You are praiseworthy, Most glorious)

Salam Right(Head Position)

Then, turn your head to the right side and say:

As-salaamu ‘alay-kum wa rah-ma-tullah

السَّلَامُ عَلَيكُم وَرَحْمَةُ اللّٰهِ

(May Allah’s peace and mercy be upon you)

Salam Left(Head Position)

Then, turn your head to the left side and say:

As-salaamu ‘alay-kum wa rah-ma-tullah

السَّلَامُ عَلَيكُم وَرَحْمَةُ اللّٰهِ
